SongSwipe is an ongoing project that allows users to swipe on cards that display another user's top 5 songs on Spotify.
Once Users match, they can view their matches and message one another.

## Built with

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org)
- [tailwindcss](https://tailwindcss.com)
- [Spotify API](https://developer.spotify.com/documentation/web-api)
- Deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com?utm_source=envshare)
